# Amiburnt

Amiburnt is a product discovery project about early burnout awareness.

The hypothesis: burnout develops gradually through subtle changes in energy, routines, sleep, focus, motivation, and rest. People often recognize the pattern only after overload has become normal.

## Vision

Help people notice burnout patterns before gradual overload becomes their baseline.

This is not a finished wellness product, fitness tracker, stress score, or productivity tool. The project is intentionally focused on understanding the problem before building a solution.

## Problem

Early burnout can be hard to recognize because the signals are easy to explain away:

- Energy feels lower, but still manageable.
- Sleep becomes less restorative.
- Routines slip.
- Focus dulls.
- Motivation drops quietly.
- Rest stops bringing people back to baseline.

Existing tools often show isolated metrics. Amiburnt explores whether people need a calmer way to notice the bigger pattern forming over time.

## Current Prototype

The landing page communicates the discovery direction and invites early research participation.

The visual concept shows a possible pattern loop: familiar baseline, subtle drift, gentle check-in, and an easier week that may help patterns return toward normal before full burnout.
