# Product brief

Amiburnt is a product discovery project focused on early burnout awareness.

## Problem
Burnout develops gradually through subtle changes in energy, routines, motivation, sleep, focus, and rest. People often recognize the pattern only after overload has started to feel normal.

## Opportunity
A calm, lightweight product experience could help people notice burnout patterns earlier without becoming another health dashboard.

## Direction
Focus on product discovery, research participation, and the story of gradual overload. Avoid positioning around stress tracking, recovery scoring, or performance optimization.
